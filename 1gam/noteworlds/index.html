Pillars

  1. To be able to use the design lenses that I've collected over the past several years (i.e. the game must also be a tool).
  2. To be my foot in the door to beginning my personal quest's stage of reification
  3. The personally reflective "note-world" aesthetic and presentation (plus the #1GAM theme of "Anthropomorphism" applied over it)

Premise = Apply My Design Toolkit

It disheartened me that, after spending so long collecting all these awesome ways to tackle making a game, I had no good way of registering the outcome of applying the myriad design lenses. Naturally then, I concluded to solve my problem by making it a game (in tandem with the OneGameAMonth.com #Anthropomorphism challenge).
